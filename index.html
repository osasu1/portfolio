About

I'm a Product Manager with a deep passion for zero-to-one product building — the messy, exhilarating work of taking something from a blank page to a product that matters. I've done this across fintech at Paxful, edtech at GritinAI, and am currently building cultural technology at Afroart Studios.

My approach is rooted in deep user empathy. I believe great products start with obsessive curiosity about the people who use them — their frustrations, motivations, and workarounds. I combine that with a structured product thinking process: discovery, prioritization, clear requirements, and relentless iteration.

I also believe in building. I use AI tools and vibe-coding to prototype quickly, validate ideas, and ship MVPs without waiting for engineering bandwidth. Standup Digest and FastIQ are products I built myself. I'm a PM who ships.

AI Productivity Tool · Async Communication
Standup Digest

A vibe-coded AI tool that converts raw standup notes into clean, structured digests. Built because async teams waste time reformatting updates. Paste in raw notes → get a polished, scannable digest instantly. Built and shipped solo using AI-assisted development.

  • Problem: Async standup updates are noisy, inconsistent, and hard to skim
  • Solution: AI reformatter that structures raw notes into digestible summaries
  • Built with: Vibe-coding + AI tools + Vercel deployment
  • Key insight: Teams need structure, not more tools — a single-action workflow wins
